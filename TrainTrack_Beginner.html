METs Reference Chart
A MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) measures how hard your body is working relative to sitting still. 1 MET = resting. 10 METs = working 10x harder than rest. Calories burned = MET × your weight (kg) × hours active.
🔵 Light <3 METs 🟡 Moderate 3–6 METs 🔴 Vigorous 6–9 METs ⚡ Max 9+ METs
